About







About Modern Business

V3 Slimcare is one amongst the best and fastest growing chains of slimming and beauty clinics, started its services in the year 2012 Feb 6th in R.T.Nagar, Bengaluru. Our services consists of skin treatment, beauty, hair care, bridal makeup, laser treatment, weight loss treatment, lipolysis, peels, stem cell therapy, lipo laser treatment and permanent hair removal in Bangalore. As a brand V3 Slim Care came up with its services in different parts of Bengaluru viz., Marathalli, BTM, Malleshwaram, Indiranagar, Jayanagar and more branches in the pipe line.

In the year 2014, V3 Slim Care extended its services out of Bengaluru as well due to the excessive demand of the services. It started its first centre in Mysore which made way to open centre in Hubli in the year 2015 and one more centre in Bengaluru in New BEL Road and further spreading the pleasant services and result oriented work in Mangalore too. The next step for V3 Slim Care would be the biggest mile stone in the history to go outside Karnataka and make its presence in Slimming and Beauty areas making "WAY TO HEALTHY LIVING".

Over the years, V3 slim Care has become a well renowned & most trusted chain in Karnataka providing effective beauty solutions & services for men, women and also children right from Weight Management to Skin & Hair Treatments, Laser permanent Hair Removal, Lipo Laser Treatment with more than 25,000 satisfied customers and have built a strong reputation for safe and result oriented services in weight loss, inch loss and beauty care. During its journey, V3 Slim Care has emerged as a leader in introducing latest world class technology and advanced treatments in Karnataka. V3 Slim Care has also brought in new treatments such as Skin treatments in other words Dermatological treatment such as PRP, Derma Roller, Peels and so on. Removing un wanted hair and also Unwanted Fat helping in figure correction. Inch loss such as Lipo Laser and LipoLysis helping in Fat reduction. V3 slim Care also helps in old age people making it a one stop place for all your weight, Skin and hair related problems.
